📈 How to Read the Trading Scanner

1. What is this scanner?

This scanner automatically analyzes market data and finds stocks that show unusual activity. Instead of checking hundreds of charts manually, the scanner highlights a few stocks that may move soon.

2. Example Scanner Card

Stock: RELIANCE
Score: 7
BreakoutProb: 72
Strength: STRONG
Signal: WATCHLIST

Each field tells you something about the stock’s condition today.

3. What does Score mean?

Score measures the strength of the setup based on volume, delivery, and price behaviour.

Score 4–5 → Normal
Score 6 → Good setup forming
Score 7 → Strong momentum
Score 8+ → Very strong setup

Focus mainly on stocks with Score 6 or higher.

4. What is Breakout Probability?

Breakout Probability estimates how likely a stock is to break an important resistance level.

50 → Early stage
60 → Possible breakout
70 → Strong candidate
80+ → Very strong breakout setup

5. What is Hidden Accumulation?

Hidden accumulation suggests that larger market participants may be quietly buying the stock.

ACCUMULATION → Institutional buying detected
Blank / NO → No strong accumulation signal

6. What do the Signals mean?

WATCHLIST → Monitor the stock tomorrow

BTST_READY → Possible Buy Today Sell Tomorrow setup

BREAKOUT_READY → Watch for breakout above resistance

7. What should you do when you see a stock?

The scanner only finds opportunities. It does not mean you should buy immediately.

Step 1 – Select 3–5 interesting stocks
Step 2 – Open the chart
Step 3 – Identify resistance and support
Step 4 – Plan entry, stop loss, and target

8. What if the stock looks unfamiliar?

Sometimes the scanner may show instruments like bonds or less common securities. If you don't recognize the instrument or the chart looks unclear, simply skip it.

9. When should you use this scanner?

Best time to use the scanner is after the market closes.

1. Update market data
2. Open the scanner
3. Review signals
4. Prepare your watchlist for the next trading day

10. Important reminder

This scanner is a decision support tool. Always confirm signals using price charts and proper risk management.
